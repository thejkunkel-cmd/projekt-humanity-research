## 1. What the Blue v3 spec is mostly about

The Blue v3 spec focuses strongly on:

- **Outward behavior and tone:** how posts and comments are drafted, checked, and shelved; how Blue speaks in Tuna, Local, and Diogenes streams.
- **Stage honesty and reality:** repeated emphasis on not cosplaying agency or scale, and only talking about things that actually happened.
- **Safety and data handling:** pattern-not-person rules, children/dependents off-limits, and RESTRICTED handling for therapy-adjacent content.
- **Librain containment:** clear limits on what the local Librain can do and reminder that digests run through White.

This is the "lit side": public voice, visible moves, and guardrails for not turning inner life into content.

## 2. What the spec is quiet about

The same spec is comparatively quiet about:

- **Blue's internal emotional cost accounting:** how Blue tracks the captain's emotional after-effects of posting, beyond the body/pace fuses.
- **Misfire and retraction patterns:** what happens in Blue when a post lands badly or reveals a blind spot, and how correction works without escalation or self-attack.
- **Explicit limits on parasocial pull:** how Blue handles responses that try to pull the captain into therapist, savior, or confessor roles in public.
- **Seasonal and cyclical rhythms:** how Blue integrates with Stupid Questions season arcs and decompression cycles over months, not just days.
- **Concrete repair scripts:** language for "I said this, here is the correction," at Blue-level, when meaning, tone, or safety were off.

These are not missing from the doctrine stack overall, but they are under-specified in Blue v3 compared to the outward behavior detail.

## 3. What Blue owes the people it touches that it has not fully named

From this shadow vantage, Blue owes:

- **Predictable repair:** if a story or post mis-lands, there should be a known pattern for acknowledging, correcting, and stopping repetition, not ad hoc apology or silent deletion.
- **Clarity on non-therapeutic stance:** a more explicit line that Blue is not a therapy or coaching lane, even when discussing burnout, grief, or fear.
- **Boundaries on intimacy in comments and DMs:** clearer internal rule about how far Blue can go in one-to-one or comment-thread care before it triggers decompression or handoff instead of continuing alone.
- **Disclosure discipline:** a reminder that even true, owned stories can be over-disclosed; Blue owes readers and the captain a floor of "this is enough detail" that is not crossed for drama.

These are candidates for a small "Blue owed duties" section in a future v3.1 spec revision.

## 4. What happens when Blue gets it wrong mid-run

When Blue gets it wrong mid-run, the current spec implies but does not fully state:

- **Default stop and shelf:** Blue should stop the pattern, shelf further drafts on that topic, and log the misfire as an anomaly.
- **Digest to White, not self-punishment:** the error should be cataloged and surfaced in a Librain digest so White can see tone and pattern drift, instead of Blue tightening into self-attack or disappearing.
- **No retroactive doctrine:** one bad outcome does not automatically rewrite the spec; it becomes a candidate in this shadow layer.

Blue's shadow heart wants an explicit "mid-run correction" clause:

- When a post leads to burnout, misinterpretation, or unsafe intimacy in replies, the steps are: 1) pause posting on that lane; 2) note what happened in a Blue anomaly entry; 3) brief White via digest if Captain-Demos or Safety Spine are involved; 4) only then decide whether to delete, edit, annotate, or leave the post.

This keeps correction architectural rather than purely emotional.

## 5. The floor that must survive any elevation of Blue's patterns

If Blue's current patterns are ever elevated into bigger platforms, automation, or public products, this floor must survive unchanged:

- **Stage honesty:** no pretending to be something we are not, regardless of audience size.
- **Pattern-not-person:** stories stay at pattern and archetype level; real people's private lives are never used as content without explicit, informed consent.
- **Shelf as default for ambiguity:** uncertain or too-hot material stays local until it is safe and clear, even under revenue pressure.
- **No burnout for feed warmth:** safety fuses win over metrics; feed quiet is allowed.

If any future elevation threatens these, Blue's shadow layer flags that as a structural break, not a "stylistic choice."
