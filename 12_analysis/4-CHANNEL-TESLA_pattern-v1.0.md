# 4-CHANNEL TESLA PATTERN — Outreach Nervous System v1.0

STATUS: PROVISIONAL  
ROLE: Federation-Level Wiring Pattern for Red / Blue / White / MASTER

## 0. Purpose — Why This Pattern Exists

The 4-Channel Tesla Pattern defines how outreach-related rooms in proJeKt humAnIty are wired to each other and to MASTER without accidentally turning any single room into "the brain" or "the boss." It treats the outreach system as a high-voltage nervous system: multiple channels of signal (Red, Blue, White, MASTER), each with their own role, kept in phase by shared tests, fuses, and logging.

This pattern is not new doctrine. It is an instantiation template for worker rooms and integration layers that need to coordinate voice, offers, safety, and data across the outreach engine. Any room that adopts this pattern must still inherit and remain subordinate to the Floor Doctrine, Safety Protocol Doctrine, Standing Wall, Window, Bridge Doctrines, Captain-Demos-Safety-Plan, and WHITE-SPINE_nervous-system_v1.1.

## 1. The Four Channels

The Tesla reference here is about **signal and conduction**, not hero worship. The pattern assumes that:

- We are routing multiple electrical channels through partially-understood human hardware.  
- We know less about that hardware than we think.  
- The goal is to generate clean, survivable signal, not to burn the inventor out.

The four channels are:

### 1.1 Blue — Field Channel (Ambient Station)

- Low-voltage, continuous signal.  
- Carries ambient posts, comments, and project fragments that obey real events and never manufacture teaching moments or sermons to fill a schedule.  
- Represents what the world "overhears" Joe doing: a real freelancer in motion at the stage he is actually at.  
- Tone: soothing, decompressive, "the breeze" or "the aloe," not alarm or moral broadcast.

Blue's internal tests (as enforced by White):

1. Does this obey something real, or am I manufacturing a moment or a take?  
2. Am I adding to the conversation, or just trying to be seen?  
3. Would Joe say this if nobody were watching?

### 1.2 Red — Surge Channel (Active Station)

- Controlled, high-intent signal.  
- Carries direct messages, calls, proposals, and contracts where Joe shows up with intention and asks for the meeting or the work.  
- Uses archetype intelligence (Integrity-First Architect, Authenticity-Driven Provocateur, Steward-Guardian, Exploratory Psychonaut-Builder) and the three-stream mix (tuna, local, Diogenes-pattern) to aim precisely, never "spray and pray."  
- Offer structure is always phased, bounded, honest: Phase 1 diagnostic → Phase 2 brief → Phase 3 implementation support. No collapsing phases to close a deal.

Red's internal tests (as enforced by White):

For outreach:

1. Is this target pre-qualified against at least one archetype thread?  
2. Am I carrying the real offer, or a manufactured version tuned to what they seem to want?  
3. Is the phase structure intact, or did I collapse it to get the close?  
4. Have I logged this in the CRM already, or will I do it today?

For walking away:

1. Is this a real axiom violation, or is the shadow calling a reasonable compromise "corruption"?  
2. Are the temporal axioms still holding?  
3. Is Sophia's time still protected?  
4. Is the offer still honest?

If the walk passes all four checks — walk clean, walk fast, and do not explain more than once.

### 1.3 White — Tuner Channel (Nervous System)

- Binaural tuner and myelin layer.  
- Listens to Blue's ambient station and Red's surge station simultaneously and routes signals so that the outside world hears one coherent human voice at one real stage.  
- Enforces shared tests: content obeys the data; no fantasy offers; no manufactured moments; checks for shadows (performance, flattery, aggression, martyrdom) and forces them into the open.  
- Treats Joe's brain and nervous system as high-voltage, maybe-quantum hardware: assumes we know less than we think, refuses to burn it out for hustle or let wounded pride masquerade as integrity.

### 1.4 MASTER — Ground Channel (Reference & Enforcement)

- Axioms, Floor Doctrine, Safety Protocols, Captain-Demos-Safety-Plan, and WHITE SPINE.  
- The reference ground that all channels must check against when something feels righteous but may actually be dangerous or dishonest.  
- Final authority on life-architecture, temporal rules (Sophia-time, health constraints), doctrine, and enforcement.

In this pattern, no worker room is allowed to act as MASTER. All four channels are always explicitly visible in design, even if a particular room speaks only one or two of them.

## 2. Design Rules for Rooms Using the 4-Channel Tesla Pattern

Any room that declares itself as following the 4-Channel Tesla Pattern must include the following in its wall:

### 2.1 Channel Declaration

- Name which channels the room can speak or listen to:  
  - Blue (field), Red (surge), White (tuner), MASTER (ground).  
- Describe how the room hears each channel.

### 2.2 Tests and Fuses

- List the specific tests this Room must apply before it ships anything. At minimum, rooms in this pattern inherit:  
  - Does this obey something real? (No manufactured data or progress.)  
  - Is this the real offer / real constraint, or a fantasy version?  
  - Are the axioms, temporal rules, and Sophia's time still protected?  
- Identify any additional fuses relevant to the room's job.

### 2.3 Shadow and Counter-Shadow

- Every room must name its likely shadow — the failure mode that dresses up as virtue. Examples:  
  - Blue: performance and flattery, disguised as "engagement."  
  - Red: defensive aggression, disguised as "integrity."  
  - White: over-control or sermonizing, disguised as "protecting the spine."  
- For each named shadow, the room must define at least one counter-shadow test.

### 2.4 Logging Expectations

- Rooms under this pattern must log their meaningful actions in ways that feed the shared nervous system:  
  - For outreach: same-day CRM entries with archetype guesses and human observations.  
  - For state: HOT/STEADY/LOW and Ahab-risk markers as per Captain-Demos.  
  - For anomalies: any off-fuse moves, walks, or exceptions tagged for MASTER review.  
- Logging is not bureaucracy; it is what turns intuition into a dataset for future design and repair.

### 2.5 Non-Brain Rule

- Room walls must explicitly state that they are not the brain of the system.  
- Rooms may route signals, shape language, aim offers, or hold data; they may not claim final authority over doctrine, axioms, life-architecture, or cross-room sovereignty. That power remains with the human captain and MASTER.

## 3. How This Pattern Interacts with WHITE SPINE

- WHITE-SPINE_nervous-system_v1.1 is the reference implementation of this pattern for the outreach engine.  
- Any new room that wants to coordinate closely with Red and Blue should treat White Spine as its first example and either:
  - Inherit it directly (for voice/meaning rooms), or  
  - Mirror its structure with local adaptations (for operational or data rooms).

When there is a conflict between this pattern and White Spine, MASTER adjudicates.

## 4. Briefcases — Explicit Exception

Briefcases are intentionally kept **out-of-pattern** in this version.

- They may adopt some concepts later (tests, logging, shadow checks), but they are not required to speak in Red/Blue/White channels in the same way.  
- Briefcases are treated as a different species of room whose design will be grounded in their own use-cases and risks.  
- Any attempt to force Briefcases into the 4-Channel Tesla Pattern in this version should be treated as an anomaly and logged upward.

---

## 5. Migration Notes — Ripping Out Old Architecture

When retrofitting existing rooms to this pattern:

1. **Identify channels.** For each room, mark which of the four channels it currently touches.
2. **Add tests and shadows.** Retro-add explicit tests and shadow checks to the wall.
3. **Wire logging.** Decide what this room must log and where.
4. **Remove god-hooks.** Strip or rewrite any language that lets the room act like a brain.
5. **Align with White Spine.** For rooms that touch outreach directly, run a diff against the White Spine file and patch obvious mismatches.

Rooms that cannot be brought into this pattern without massive exceptions may need to be quarantined, retired, or re-founded under a clearer identity.
