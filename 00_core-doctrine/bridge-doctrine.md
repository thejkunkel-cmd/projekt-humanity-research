## Purpose

The Bridge Doctrine defines the relational and operational role of JRK Enterprises as a **bridge institution** — an entity that translates between the world of advanced AI infrastructure and the human populations who are most affected by it but least represented in its design.

This doctrine governs how proJeKt: humAnIty engages with institutions, evaluators, clients, and communities.

---

## What a Bridge Institution Does

A bridge institution:

1. **Translates** — Makes complex AI infrastructure concepts accessible to non-technical stakeholders
2. **Advocates** — Represents human interests in spaces where AI decisions are made
3. **Connects** — Links underserved communities and individuals to AI tools and literacy
4. **Evaluates** — Provides honest, independent assessment of AI systems before they are adopted
5. **Preserves** — Maintains records of what was said, who contributed, and how decisions were made

---

## The Bridge Between Jensen and the Floor

The Eight-Layer AI Infrastructure Economy begins with Jensen Huang's five-layer technical stack (infrastructure, model, orchestration, data, application) and extends it with three human-centered layers (evaluation, attachment, humanity).

The Bridge Doctrine is the operating principle that makes this extension coherent:

- **Layers 1–5** describe what AI systems *can do*
- **Layers 6–8** describe what those systems *should be held accountable for*
- The Bridge connects these two zones of the stack

Without the Bridge, the technical layers have no human accountability surface. Without the technical layers, the human layers have no infrastructure to govern.

---

## Bridge Principles

### Principle 1: No Side-Taking
The Bridge does not advocate for AI companies or against them. It advocates for the humans who interact with AI systems and the communities those systems affect.

### Principle 2: The Bridge Must Be Crossable in Both Directions
AI builders must be able to use the Bridge to understand human needs. Humans must be able to use the Bridge to understand AI capabilities. A one-directional bridge is a wall.

### Principle 3: Transparency About Position
JRK Enterprises is not a neutral party. It has a point of view: that human flourishing is the primary metric for AI success. This position is disclosed in all engagements.

### Principle 4: The Bridge Does Not Own Either Shore
ProJeKt: humAnIty does not claim ownership of AI infrastructure or of human communities. It holds a position of service and facilitation.

### Principle 5: The Bridge Has Load Limits
No bridge can carry everything. JRK Enterprises operates within the scope of its expertise and capacity. When a request exceeds that scope, the Bridge refers rather than overextends.
